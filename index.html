The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and services for citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Empowerment

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus, the Nobel laureate and founder of Grameen Bank, has a vision for Bangladesh that focuses on unity, transparency, and democratic renewal. In his speeches, he emphasizes the need for national unity, combating corruption, and overhauling key sectors like the judiciary, banking, and education. He envisions a Bangladesh where democracy is restored, and the sacrifices made by the people translate into a just and inclusive society.
